About Me

I am a tech-driven healthcare professional currently pursuing my Bachelor of Engineering (BE) at Global Academy of Technology (2026), with a Diploma in Computer Science (PES Polytechnic, 2023, CGPA: 8.05). My unique blend of technical expertise in Python, Machine Learning, and Data Analysis and hands-on experience as an Insurance Approver at Suvarna Arogya Suraksha Trust allows me to bridge healthcare systems and technology. Passionate about data-driven solutions, I leverage tools like Tableau, Power BI, and DBMS to streamline processes, while my volunteer work as a COVID Warrior (BBMP) and Medical Camp Organizer (Krishna Vokkaligara Sangha) reflects my commitment to equitable healthcare access.

Beyond my professional roles, I thrive on solving complex problems at the intersection of healthcare and technology. Whether optimizing insurance approvals through data analysis or building ML models to predict medical trends, I believe in using technology to create inclusive systems. My work is driven by values of empathy, precision, and social impact—principles I honed while serving vulnerable communities during the pandemic. When not coding or reviewing claims, you’ll find me contributing to open-source projects on GitHub or mentoring peers in data science.
